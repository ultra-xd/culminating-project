Aaron's Funny Fighting Game


How to Play:

Press the WASD keys to move your character. Press left-click on an enemy to swing your sword at an enemy. Press right-click to create a sweeping attack around you that damages all enemies around you. Zombies slowly chase you around relentlessly, while skeletons are fast and shoot you with their arrows. Fireball towers will launch fireballs at you. Survive as long as you can!
